The Life of Patrick

Patrick Star is a highly coveted actor "starring" in the show SpongeBob SquarePants. His rise to fame showed all starfish that anything is possible .

Born on August 17th, Star was born under a rock in Bikini Bottom. From a young age, naive Patrick loved messing with Squidward with the help of his bestfriend and neighbor SpongeBob. This website shows the many photgraphs of Patrick Star and his adventures in Bikini Bottom
